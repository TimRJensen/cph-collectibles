Your number one currator of historic memorabilia

Our ambition is simple: to curate rare items of genuine historic significance.

To achieve this, we have partnered with Nikolaj E. Mortensen — a prolific figure within historic racing. Over the past two decades, his journey has taken him from amateur driver to partner of Jan Magnussen, and from entrepreneur to one of Denmark’s leading authorities in the restoration of veteran automobiles.

With deep roots in the historic racing community and a network that spans the sport, Nikolaj brings both authenticity and expertise to Copenhagen Collectables. We are proud to have him as part of our story.
